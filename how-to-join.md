# How to Join

Great — you'd like to be part of the community! The simplest way to join is:

1. Join our Discord server.
   - Use the invite link here: https://discord.gg/PjN32EmSDr
   - If you don't have a Discord account, sign up at https://discord.com and then follow the invite link.

2. Contact us by email.
   - Send a message to: chennaiinnovatorsnetwork@gmail.com
   - Tell us who you are, what you'd like to do, and any relevant links (GitHub, portfolio, topic of interest).

That's it — joining Discord and emailing us is sufficient to become part of the project/community.

## Quick message template

Use this short template when emailing or posting on Discord if you want:

Hello — my name is [Your Name]. I'd like to join and contribute to the project. I am interested in [areas of interest]. You can find my work at [link] */optional/*. How can I get started?

Thanks,
[Your Name]

## Etiquette & expectations

- Be respectful and constructive in Discord.
- Read pinned channels or the onboarding channel for project-specific guidelines.
- Expect a reply within a few days; if you don't hear back, follow up politely.

## Privacy

We only use the email you provide to contact you about the project. If you have privacy concerns, tell us in your message.

## Need help?

If you don't have the invite link or you're unsure where to send mail, open an issue in this repo or look for contact details in the project `README.md`.
